i research and design.

how i work

my process.

01 — Discover
frame the questions

Align on what's worth researching. Stakeholder interviews, desk research, competitive analysis and scoping workshops to make sure we're solving the right problem.

02 — listen
understand the user

Talk to users, observe real workflows, and capture the signals behind their behavior. Through interviews, shadowing, and qualitative research, I gather the evidence needed to understand what people actually do — not just what they say.

03 — synthesize
find the pattern

Thematic analysis, affinity diagrams, insight clustering. I translate qualitative richness into clear, actionable findings that teams can actually act on.

04 — deliver
drive real change

Reports, workshops, and design crit sessions. I stay involved through implementation and measure impact with post-launch research to close the loop.
